Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe



How to make Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich.

Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Cheddar (Other delicious options are Gruyere, Brie, Fontina, Gouda, Pepper Jack, and havarti)

  • Plain white sandwich bread(You could also use sourdough, brioche, or even ciabatta)

Grilled Cheese Sandwich Step-By-Step

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.

  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).

  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.

  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
